# 🛰️ S30-PRO Federation: Master Workflow
> **Version:** 1.5.0 (Garmt-Factory Standard)
> **Objective:** Deterministic pipeline from AAVSO Haul to Photon Acquisition.

## 🏁 PHASE 1: THE FUNNEL (Pre-Flight Data Factory)

1. **THE HARVEST**
   - **Script:** `core/preflight/harvester.py`
   - **Action:** Submits Seestar FOV to AAVSO.
   - **Output:** `data/campaign_targets.json`

   - **Script:** `core/preflight/librarian.py`
   - **Action:** Sanitizes malformed AAVSO entries; fetches/attaches comparison star data.
   - **Output:** `data/targets.json` (The Master Catalog)

3. **THE GRIP VALIDATION**
   - **Script:** `core/preflight/asassn_validator.py`
   - **Action:** Cross-references targets against available comparison charts.
   - **Output:** `data/observable_targets.json` (The "Ready for Science" list)

4. **THE REFINEMENT (Daily @ 17:00)**
   - **Script:** `core/preflight/nightly_planner.py`
   - **Action:** Filters by live GPS, >30° Altitude, and Astronomical Dark window.
   - **Output:** `data/tonights_plan.json` (The Final Slew List)

## 🛡️ PHASE 2: THE HANDOVER (The Gatekeeper)

5. **PHYSICAL AUDIT**
   - **Script:** `core/flight/preflight_check.py`
   - **Check:** Storage Sentinel + Temporal Alignment (PPS) + Spatial Lock (GPS).

6. **METEOROLOGICAL AUDIT**
   - **Script:** `core/preflight/weather_audit.py`
   - **Check:** Live Cloud Cover % vs. Safety Limit.

7. **MISSION CONTROL**
   - **Script:** `core/flight/orchestrator.py`
   - **Action:** Executes the ET Protocol (PUT) using `tonights_plan.json`.
